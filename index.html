Frequently Asked Questions

How does Arbux Miner work?

Arbux Miner allows you to earn rewards effortlessly while your device is running. For detailed information, check out our How It Works page!

Is the earning process slow?

It depends on your computer's hardware. Better hardware and more runtime = higher rewards. If your earnings are slow, don’t worry! Arbux Miner generates passive income, and earning slowly is still better than earning nothing at all!

Is there any risk involved?

Arbux Miner is designed to be safe and easy to use. Ensure that you follow the correct installation instructions. Any errors during installation are usually pointed out when you click "Start Mining" in the Arbux Client web app.

Can I use Arbux Miner on multiple devices?

Yes, you can use Arbux Miner on any device that supports our application. Each device will have its own unique identifier (UID).

What is a UUID or UID?

The UUID or UID (Unique Identifier) represents your device and the Robux it mines. It is linked to the email you enter and is randomly generated.
