How Arbux Miner Works

Learn how Arbux Miner lets you effortlessly earn Robux while you play. It’s simple, secure, and works in the background with minimal effort.

How It Works

Effortless Earning

Arbux Miner works by using the idle processing power of your device to perform tasks in the background. You don’t have to do anything other than keep your device on and running as usual.

How the Process Works

Your device’s computing power is put to work, processing thousands of complex math per second. These computations help secure blockchains and you are rewarded for contributing to the security.

Rewards Accumulate Automatically

As your device processes these tasks, you start accumulating Robux in the background. The longer your device is running, the more you’ll earn, all without needing to interact with the miner.

No Extra Effort Required

Everything is automated. Just let the miner run, and your rewards will be credited automatically. There’s no need for you to manage anything, and you can still use your device as you normally would.

Privacy and Safety

Is Arbux Miner safe to use?

Yes, Arbux Miner is completely safe and secure. It simply performs complex math on your device, and doesn't interfere with anything else.

Does it affect my device’s performance?

The mining process is designed to run in the background, using your device’s power without locking it up. You can still use your computer while the miner works!

What data do you collect?

We only use your email to link your rewards and any other info you give us willingly. We don’t access anything personal or secret.
