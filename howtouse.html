Start Earning Robux with Arbux Miner!

Follow these simple steps to begin using Arbux Miner and start mining Robux today.

Step 1: Download and Install

  • Visit the official Arbux Miner website to download the app.
  • Open the installer and follow the on-screen instructions to complete the installation.
  • Once installed, launch the app to get started.

Step 2: Link Your Email Address

  • Upon the first run after installation, you will be prompted to enter your email address.
  • Your device will be given a new, fresh unique ID or UID which will be linked to your email address. All your earnings on that device will be linked to that UID.

Step 3: Start Mining

  • Click on the “Start Mining” button to begin the process.
  • Your device will automatically start working to mine Robux passively.
  • You can minimize the app and use your computer normally while the mining process runs in the background.

Step 4: Track Your Earnings

  • As your device mines, you’ll accumulate credits.
  • Check your progress through the website to see how much you’ve earned so far.
  • The longer your device works and the better your device is, the more credits you’ll earn!

Step 5: Cash Out Your Robux

  • Once you reach the minimum payout threshold, you can cash out your earnings.
  • Follow the steps in the website to withdraw your Robux securely.
  • You can cash out the earnings associated with each UID linked to your email. Therefore, avoid linking your UID to someone else's email unless you intend to gift your earnings to them.
  • Enjoy your Robux and keep mining to earn more!
